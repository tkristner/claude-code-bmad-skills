# Step 1: Problem & Opportunity Discovery

**Step:** 1 of 4
**Purpose:** Understand the problem space and identify the opportunity
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes
**Entry Criteria:** User has a product idea or problem to explore

---

## Objectives

Capture: Problem statement, Opportunity description, Initial project name, Context

---

## Discovery Questions

### Problem Space

1. **What problem are you trying to solve?** (pain points, frustrations)
2. **Who experiences this problem?** (initial audience, frequency)
3. **What happens if unsolved?** (consequences, why now)
4. **Have others tried solving this?** (existing solutions, limitations)

### Opportunity Space

5. **What does success look like?** (ideal end state, measurable improvements)
6. **What makes this opportunity unique?** (competitive landscape, differentiation)

---

## Facilitator Instructions

1. Start open: "Tell me about the problem you want to solve"
2. Use active listening - reflect back what you hear
3. Ask follow-ups to deepen understanding
4. Avoid jumping to solutions too early

**Probing Techniques:**
- 5 Whys for root cause
- "Tell me more about..."
- "Can you give an example?"
